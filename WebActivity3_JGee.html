Polar Bears

Polar Bears are one of the biggest land mammals on the planet. They weigh between 900 to 1,600 pounds and eat other animals. Here are some fun facts about Polar Bears: The liver of a polar bear holds so much vitamin A that if a human were to eat it they would die. The Polar Bears Fur is naturally clear but because of the snowly climate they live in it appears white. The Polar Bear has black skin. Polar bears are 7ft 9in when standing up.

Home > Baboon

Baboons are one of the most dangerous animals on the planet. Although they look funny and are not big these animals are freakishly strong with sharp 7 inch teeth. Baboon alphas are distiguishable from the rest of the pack due to their bright colored faces and their bright red butts. Baboons weigh about 23 pounds but are very strong and fast. Baboons are very territorial and if you see one in the wild stay away from it.
